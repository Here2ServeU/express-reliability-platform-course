# High-Demand AIOps Engineer Blueprint

## What Hiring Teams Expect
- Strong observability fundamentals (metrics, logs, events, traces)
- Fast incident triage with clear communication
- Practical risk scoring and prioritization
- Automation for repeatable incident response
- Evidence-driven post-incident reviews

## Skills You Practice in V8
- Build detection rules from SLO/SLI signals
- Score incidents with transparent logic
- Generate machine-readable incident summaries
- Compare AIOps recommendations against runbooks
- Promote validated tests from dev to staging to prod

## Portfolio Evidence Checklist
- At least 3 incident patterns with risk logic
- Local and cloud incident test reports
- One incident summary artifact per test
- Recovery metrics tied to SLO/SLI targets
- Post-incident action items and owners
